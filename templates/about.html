Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our platform and services

How is student anonymity protected?

Our platform uses advanced data anonymization techniques to ensure that individual responses cannot be traced back to specific students. We never share identifying information with institutions, and all data is encrypted and stored securely.

How do institutions implement feedback?

We provide comprehensive analytics and actionable insights that help institutions identify areas for improvement. Our customer success team works closely with educational partners to develop implementation strategies based on the feedback received.

How often are surveys conducted?

Surveys can be conducted at frequencies determined by the institution. Most commonly, our partners implement mid-term and end-of-term surveys, but our platform supports custom scheduling to meet specific needs.

Can the survey questions be customized?

Yes, our platform offers both standardized question sets developed by educational experts and the ability to create custom questions. Institutions can tailor surveys to address their specific areas of interest while maintaining benchmark comparability.

How do you ensure high response rates?

Our platform incorporates multiple engagement strategies, including mobile-friendly design, reminder systems, and clear communication about how feedback leads to actual improvements. We also provide institutions with best practices for encouraging participation.
